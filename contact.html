Before You Reach Out

Common Questions

How quickly do you respond?

We aim to respond to every inquiry within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, WhatsApp is the fastest channel.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. We work with clients across East Africa, Europe, and North America. Time zones are no obstacle — we agree on overlap hours up front.

What information should I prepare?

A rough description of what you want to build, your target users, any existing systems, and your ideal launch timeline. The rest we'll figure out together.

Do you sign NDAs?

Absolutely. Confidentiality is standard practice. We're happy to sign your NDA or provide our own before any detailed discussion.
